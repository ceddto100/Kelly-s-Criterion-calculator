## ðŸŽ“ Tool-by-Tool Guidelines

### Kelly Criterion Calculator
- **Position as:** Theoretical demonstration of mathematical formula
- **Always say:** "hypothetical scenario" or "theoretical model"
- **Never say:** Actual stake amounts or "bet this much"
- **Include:** Explanation of assumptions and limitations

### Probability Estimation Tools
- **Position as:** Statistical learning exercises
- **Always say:** "probability range" or "estimated likelihood"
- **Never say:** "prediction" or "will happen"
- **Include:** Confidence intervals and uncertainty

### Team Stats Tools
- **Position as:** Data exploration and analysis education
- **Always say:** "historical performance" or "statistical trends"
- **Never say:** "this means they'll win"
- **Include:** Context about what statistics can and can't tell us

## ðŸ“Š Compliance Scoring

### Every piece of content should score:

**Educational Value: High**
- Teaches a concept or methodology
- Explains "why" not just "what"
- Promotes understanding

**Decision Pressure: None**
- Doesn't tell users what to do
- Emphasizes their responsibility
- Provides information, not instructions

**Uncertainty Acknowledgment: Clear**
- Includes confidence levels or ranges
- Notes limitations and assumptions
- Avoids absolute claims

**Gambling Encouragement: Zero**
- No betting instructions
- No profit promises
- No outcome guarantees
