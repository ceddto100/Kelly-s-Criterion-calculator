1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Betgistics ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, stores, shares, and protects your personal information when you use our Android mobile application and associated services hosted at betgistics.com.

By downloading, installing, or using Betgistics, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the App.

This policy applies to all users of the Betgistics Android application available on the Google Play Store.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Collected Through Google Sign-In

When you authenticate using Google OAuth 2.0, we receive and store the following information from your Google account:

Data Type Source Purpose
Full name Google account profile Account identification and display
Email address Google account profile Account identification, communication, and login
Profile picture (avatar) Google account profile User interface personalization

We access only the basic profile scopes required for authentication. We do not request access to your Google contacts, calendar, files, or any other Google services beyond your basic profile information.

Through your use of the App, you may provide the following information:

Data Type Purpose Storage

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

Betgistics does not collect, access, or store any of the following:

  • Precise or approximate device location / GPS data
  • Contacts or address book information
  • Phone number, call logs, or SMS/MMS messages
  • Device microphone or camera data
  • Files or documents on your device
  • Health, fitness, or biometric data
  • Advertising identifiers for ad targeting

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect limited technical information to maintain and improve the App:

  • Device type and operating system version -- for compatibility and debugging purposes
  • App version -- to ensure you have the latest features and security updates
  • Crash reports and error logs -- to identify and resolve technical issues

This technical data is collected in aggregate form and is not linked to your personal identity.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  1. Account Management: Create, authenticate, and maintain your user account via Google OAuth
  2. Core Functionality: Store and display your bet logs, bankroll data, and Kelly Criterion calculations
  3. AI Analysis: Process your matchup queries through Google Gemini AI to provide analytical insights
  4. Payment Processing: Facilitate transactions through Stripe for premium features or subscriptions
  5. Service Improvement: Diagnose technical issues and improve App performance and reliability
  6. Communication: Send service-related notices such as security alerts, account notifications, or policy updates

We do NOT use your data for:

  • Targeted advertising or behavioral profiling
  • Sale or rental to third parties
  • Marketing by unrelated third parties
  • Automated decision-making that produces legal effects

4. Third-Party Services

Betgistics integrates with the following third-party services. Each service receives only the minimum data necessary to perform its function:

4.1 Google OAuth 2.0 (Authentication)

  • Data shared: Authentication tokens exchanged during the sign-in flow
  • Purpose: Securely authenticate your identity without requiring a separate password
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Google Gemini AI (Matchup Analysis)

  • Data shared: Matchup query details, betting odds, and related context you provide for analysis
  • Purpose: Generate AI-powered analytical insights for sports matchups
  • Data handling: Query data is sent to Google Gemini API in real time for processing. We do not send personally identifiable information (such as your name or email) to the Gemini API.
  • Privacy Policy: Google Privacy Policy
  • AI Terms: Google Generative AI Terms of Service

4.3 Stripe (Payment Processing)

  • Data shared: Email address and payment details you enter during checkout
  • Purpose: Securely process payments for premium features or subscriptions
  • Data handling: All payment card information is collected and processed directly by Stripe. We do not receive, store, or have access to your full credit card number, CVV, or other sensitive payment credentials. We receive only a transaction confirmation and a tokenized reference.
  • Privacy Policy: Stripe Privacy Policy

4.4 MongoDB (Data Storage)

  • Data stored: Account profile data, bet history, and bankroll data
  • Purpose: Persistent storage of your account and betting data
  • Data handling: Data is stored on secure, access-controlled MongoDB servers. Database access is restricted to authorized backend services only.

5. Data Retention

5.1 Account and Betting Data

We retain your account profile, bet logs, and bankroll data for as long as your account remains active. This data is necessary to provide the core functionality of the App.

5.2 AI Query Data

Matchup analysis queries sent to Google Gemini AI are processed in real time and are not permanently stored by Betgistics after the response is delivered. Google may retain data according to their own data retention policies.

5.3 Payment Data

Transaction records (excluding full card details) are retained as required for accounting, tax compliance, and dispute resolution, typically for a period of up to 7 years in accordance with applicable financial regulations.

5.4 Technical Logs

Crash reports and error logs are retained for up to 90 days and are then automatically purged.

5.5 After Account Deletion

Upon account deletion (see Section 6), we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes such as fraud prevention or financial record-keeping.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between the App, our backend servers, and third-party services uses TLS/HTTPS encryption
  • Encryption at rest: Sensitive data stored in our database is encrypted at rest
  • Access controls: Database and server access is restricted to authorized personnel and services only
  • Authentication security: Google OAuth 2.0 eliminates the need to store passwords, reducing credential-related risks
  • Payment security: All payment data is handled by Stripe, a PCI DSS Level 1 certified payment processor
  • Input validation: All user inputs are validated and sanitized to prevent injection attacks
  • Regular updates: We apply security patches and updates to our infrastructure on a regular basis

While we employ robust security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
