## ðŸŽ¯ Success Criteria

The Betgistics compliance framework succeeds when:

1. **Clear Educational Positioning**
   - App is consistently described as educational
   - No confusion with gambling applications
   - Platform reviewers understand the purpose

2. **Safe AI Behavior**
   - AI never encourages gambling
   - AI never guarantees outcomes
   - AI always emphasizes learning
   - AI includes appropriate disclaimers

3. **Compliant Language**
   - No prohibited terms in outputs
   - Educational vocabulary used consistently
   - Disclaimers present where needed
   - Uncertainty acknowledged

4. **User Understanding**
   - Users understand it's for learning
   - Users don't expect betting advice
   - Users know they're responsible for decisions
   - Users recognize limitations

5. **Platform Approval**
   - Approved in Education category
   - No policy violations
   - Passes platform safety reviews
   - Maintains good standing
